Penguado Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 7 June 2025
Last Updated: 17 August 2025

At Penguado, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to keep it secure when you use our services, including our proxy features and account‑based offerings.


Information We Collect


Account Information

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Password (stored securely as a SHA‑256 hash)
  • Network IP address (used internally for operational purposes only; not tied to searches)
  • A unique user identifier (UUID) generated by Penguado


Usage and Activity Data

  • Searches you perform
  • Videos you play and download
  • Games you interact with on our platform
  • Whether you use our Google feature (operates through a secure proxy and does not expose browsing data)

Logs are tied to your UUID, not your name or IP address. To ensure your privacy and anonymity, we do not track your name or IP for searches.


Support and Communication

If you contact us for support, we collect your email address and any information you provide in your inquiry.


Cookies and Local Storage

We use cookies and local storage to store UUIDs and emails to keep you logged in and maintain your session securely. Cookies are not used for tracking beyond this scope.


How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and maintain your Penguado account and services
  • To personalize and improve your user experience
  • To analyze site traffic, usage patterns, and optimize our platform using Google Analytics
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security threats
  • To enforce our Terms of Service and flag or remove users who violate our policies


Data Retention

Account and personal data are stored indefinitely until you request deletion.
Usage logs and activity data are retained for one year and then permanently deleted.


Your Rights and Data Access

  • A copy of your personal data we hold
  • Deletion of your account and associated personal data

Requests can be sent to: penguado.web@gmail.com


Data Security

Strong encryption protocols across all services and data storage.
Access to user data restricted solely to the site developer.
We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.


Third‑Party Services

Penguado uses third‑party services such as Google Analytics for website performance tracking and Google Drive for verification code sharing. These services are bound by their own privacy policies.


Proxy Services

Our proxy features are designed for privacy and anonymity. We do not log, track, or store the websites you visit.
Instead, all proxy activity is linked only to your UUID. Your name and IP address are never tracked for searches.


No Minimum Age Requirement

Penguado does not impose a minimum age requirement to use its services.


Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new “Last Updated” date.
